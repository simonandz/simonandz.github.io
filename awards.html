Awards & Honors

Recognitions and achievements throughout my high school journey

  • 2nd Place – Bergen Tech Hackathon
    Apr 2025

    In a 12-hour Hackathon, created a project to read basic single-frame sign language signals by mapping hand points and using a machine-learning model to predict their meanings.

  • 2nd Runner Up North America – MIT Engineers Without Borders
    Mar 2025

    Participated in an assessment on engineering topics (both theoretical and application-based). Won 2nd Runner Up in the United States and North America.

  • 3rd Place Technical Design Report – Seaperch
    Mar 2025

    Competed in an ROV competition and authored a 10-page technical design report detailing methodology and data analysis techniques. Won 3rd Place for the design report.

  • Spencer Scholar – Spencer Savings Bank
    Mar 2025

    One of only two Governor’s STEM Scholars selected to meet with Spencer Savings Bank and receive $10,000 on behalf of Governor’s STEM Scholars.

  • Platinum – Cyber Patriots
    Dec 2024

    Achieved the Platinum Division (top 25% of all participants) in the Cyber Patriots competition, representing Bergen County Technical High School – Teterboro.

  • Finalist – Bronze Honour
    International Astronomy & Astrophysics Competition
    Jun 2024

    Finalist in the International Astronomy & Astrophysics Competition, solving both mathematics and research-based questions. Placed in the top 25% of all contestants.

  • 3rd Place – Seaperch NJ/NY Regional Mission Course
    Mar 2024

    Designed and piloted a remotely operated vehicle with a team, competing at the Seaperch NJ/NY Regional. Won 3rd Place in the Mission Course.

  • Best Climate Project – Bergen Tech Hackathon
    Mar 2024

    In a 12-hour Hackathon, created a tool using Google Maps API and Distance Matrix API to calculate CO₂ emissions for various transportation modes and recommend the most eco-friendly option.

  • 6th Place – TEAMs International Competition (Build & Design)
    Jul 2023

    Qualified through rounds requiring an engineered solution, a conceptual essay on bike design and safety, and a Mathematics & Science exam. At the international event, my team placed 6th in Build & Design.

  • The President’s Gold Volunteer Service Award
    Jun 2023

    Volunteered over 200 hours with Camerata Youth Orchestra, earning the President’s Gold Award through AmeriCorps for exceptional community service.
