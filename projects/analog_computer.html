Analog Computer for Damped Harmonic Motion

Objective

Model the second-order damped‐spring oscillator differential equation using analog op‐amp circuits and verify behavior on an oscilloscope.

Outcome & Contribution

• Created and simulated a SPICE model representing the damped harmonic motion circuit.
• Iterated on component values to match theoretical damping ratio and natural frequency.
• Measured waveforms on an oscilloscope, confirming expected exponential decay.
• Wrote a detailed lab report including transfer function derivation and SPICE netlist.

Skills & Tools

  • SPICE (PSpice/Multisim modeling)
  • Analog Circuit Design (op‐amps, RC networks)
  • Circuit Analysis (transfer function derivation)
  • Oscilloscope Measurements & Lab Reporting
