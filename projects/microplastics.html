NASA Microplastics Density Experiment

Objective

Measure the frequency of microplastics in the stratosphere by designing a lightweight filter payload to fly on a NASA high-altitude balloon.

Outcome & Contribution

• Led the team to finalize mechanical mounting and filter housing design in Fusion 360.
• Designed custom PCB with CircuitPython microcontroller for sampling pump control and data logging.
• Coordinated power-management system to regulate pump voltage and sensor readout.
• Preparation complete; launch scheduled for May–June on NASA HRPB (high-altitude research balloon).

Skills & Tools

  • Fusion 360 (mechanical CAD for payload housing)
  • CircuitPython (firmware for microcontroller logging)
  • Microcontrollers & Sensors (pump control, environmental sensing)
  • Power Management (voltage regulators, battery selection)
