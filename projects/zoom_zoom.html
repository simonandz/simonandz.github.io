Zoom Zoom CPU & Keccak f1600 Accelerator

Objective

Zoom Zoom’s goal was to build a custom 16-bit CPU featuring a unique opcode set, memory management unit, and an integrated Keccak-f1600 accelerator core. Final fabrication occurred as part of TinyTapeOut’s TT08 multi-project wafer run.

Outcome & Contribution

• Designed and verified the entire CPU datapath and control logic in Verilog.
• Implemented handshake lines and data transfers between main memory and the Keccak f1600 accelerator.
• Successfully synthesized the design, taped out on TT08, and validated basic operation post-fabrication.
• Worked closely with a four-member team to integrate the Verilog IP with the wafer foundry toolchain.

Skills & Tools

  • Verilog (RTL coding, testbenches)
  • Digital Electronics (CPU microarchitecture)
  • ASIC Design (TinyTapeOut flow, foundry PDK)
  • Team Collaboration (Git, version control)
